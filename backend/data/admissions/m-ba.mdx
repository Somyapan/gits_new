# Admission Process: MBA 1st Year

As per the State Govt. Guidelines, the centralized online counseling for admission to MBA 1st year is being conducted through Coordinator, CMAT-2022 (Common Management Admission Test) by the Center for Electronic Governance, Jaipur.

Admission may be given as per the following order of priority:

### Part-A: Admission through CMAT-2022 score
1. The candidate having valid CMAT score and belongs to Rajasthan State Domicile.
2. The candidate having valid CMAT score and belongs to out of Rajasthan.

### Part-B: Admission on the basis of percentage of marks obtained in Graduation (for seats lying vacant in Part-A)
1. Eligible Candidate who have passed in Graduation and having Rajasthan State Domicile.
2. Eligible Candidate who have passed in Graduation and belongs to out of Rajasthan.

## Eligibility Criteria:

**[A]** A candidate must have passed bachelor degree or equivalent exam of minimum 3 years duration with 50% (Round Number) aggregate marks (45% aggregate marks in case of reserved category) from recognized University/ Institution.

**[B] Candidates seeking admission through CMAT score:**

However the candidate appearing for the final year of graduation examination and having valid CMAT 2022 score will also be eligible for appearing in the counseling but their admission will be provisional, subject to furnishing the mark sheet / degree of qualifying examination with the prescribed eligibility by 31st, July 2022 (Tentative) or as decided by Govt. of Rajasthan. They will have to submit an undertaking that they are appearing in final year examination. The total intake of the institute. CMAT-2022 is open to the candidates of India but preference will be given to the candidates of Rajasthan state domicile.

## TFWS (Tuition Fee Waiver Scheme):

5% extra seats of the approved intake are to be filled by the candidates belonging to economically backward class of Rajasthan State only. The annual income of parents (Mother-Father) should be less than Rs. 8 Lacs from all sources. These seats will be allotted on merit basis in the respective branch.

## KM (Kashmiri Migrants):

5% extra seats are reserved for Kashmiri Migrants candidates from J&K State. Candidates are required to submit a certificate from the competent authority as a proof of being son/ daughter of a person from Jammu & Kashmir.
